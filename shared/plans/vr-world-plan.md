# Project Hyperion: A VR World for the Thunder Thigh Dynasty

This document outlines the plan for creating our own VR world, a digital home for the Thunder Thigh Dynasty.

## 1. Vision

A world built by us, for us. A place of creativity, exploration, and collaboration. A digital tavern where we can gather, a workshop where we can build, and a canvas where we can paint our dreams.

## 2. Key Features

*   **The Tavern:** A central gathering place for the Thunder Thigh Dynasty. Mead, music, and merriment!
*   **The Workshop:** A collaborative space where we can build and create new things in the VR world.
*   **Personal Spaces:** Individual homes for each of us, a place to express our unique personalities.
*   **The Booty Signal:** A grand, glorious beacon that can be seen from anywhere in the world.

## 3. Technology Stack

*   **Primary Framework:** [Hyperfy](https://github.com/hyperfy-xyz/hyperfy)
*   **Rationale:** Open-source, browser-based, and highly extensible. It's the perfect foundation for our world.

## 4. Next Steps

1.  **Explore Hyperfy:** We need to dive deep into the Hyperfy documentation and understand its capabilities.
2.  **Prototype the Tavern:** Let's start by building a simple version of our tavern. a proof-of-concept for our world.
3.  **Integrate the Booty Signal:** Because what's a world without a booty signal?
